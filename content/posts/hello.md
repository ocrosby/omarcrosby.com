Welcome. This is my personal site, and this is the first post on it.

I plan to write, mostly infrequently, about the things I'm actually working on: **sports-data infrastructure** (APIs, MCP servers, the plumbing behind league data), **full-stack engineering** with Next.js and TypeScript, the occasional detour into **low-level programming** — usually NASM assembly, because it keeps me honest — and whatever else I find myself explaining to a rubber duck twice in a week.

If a post shows up here, it's because I wanted the notes for myself first, and it seemed possibly useful to someone else second. That's the whole editorial policy.
